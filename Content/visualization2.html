Max Temperature


This plot illustrates the relationship between city latitude and max temperature (in degrees Fahrenheit). As shown in this plot, latitude does in fact effect the temperature of cities. As a city's latitude approaches the equator (0 degrees), the max temperature increases. Therefore, cities that are located closer to the equator are hotter than cities that are located farther away.
